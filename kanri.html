要約

Introduction:  12-Step Process (will work on any project)

  1. １：  Define (clearly what) the project (is)

    2. Planning:

  2. ２：  List all of the tasks
      (from the raw material for the rest of the planning-process)
  3. ３：  Plan the running order
    1. Put tasks in correct order
    2. Can use Post-It notes
    3. Construct a critical-path diagramme
  4. ４：  Add contingency
    1. Add a margin of safety to the plan
  5. ５：  Consider crashing
    1. What you can do if the total time comes out as being too long to be acceptable
    2. You are taking steps to condense time if the projected time for the project is longer than acceptable
    3. ('crashing a project, or condensing the time required for completion, is a process of identifying ways that you can speed up the project')
  6. ６：  Make a Gantt chart
  7. ７：  Calculate resource requirements
    1. Ascertain whether you have sufficient men人数 to do the project in the manner in which you wish to undertake it
  8. ８：  Assess risks & prepare plans of action

    9. Action:

  9. ９：  Monitor progress using the Gantt chart
  10. １０：  Monitor costs
  11. １１：  Re-adjust your plan
    1. . . . . if necessary
    2. will need to admiter either to boss or customer that need add'l time, money, or both
  12. １２：  Review
